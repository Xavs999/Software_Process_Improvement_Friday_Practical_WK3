# Core Game Overview

## Game Type
- Survival
- Board Game
- 1 vs Many Players (Fixed: 4 players total)

---

# Game Start

- Each player begins with **25 Victory Points**
- All players start **scattered** across different starting tiles on the board
- A shared **Question Deck** is placed at the center

---

# Win Condition

## 🎯 Goal
- First player to reach **50 Victory Points** wins

## 🏆 Ways to Earn Points
- Landing on Good Tiles
- Winning player‑vs‑player roll‑offs
- Completing Mini‑Game Question challenges
- (More point sources can be added later)

---

# Dice System

- Players roll **2 × 6‑sided dice**
- **Roll Total > 6** → Player is allowed to take an action this turn  
  (Movement, interaction, mini‑game, etc.)

---

# Board Action Tiles

## 🟥 Bad Tiles
- **Penalty on next roll**  
  (e.g., –2 to next dice total)
- **Ambush**  
  - Lose **2 Victory Points**
- **Move Backwards**  
  - Move back 1–3 spaces (your choice or fixed by tile)

---

## 🟩 Good Tiles
- **Steal Points**  
  - Roll 1 die  
  - Steal that many points from any player
- **Gain Points**  
  - Roll 1 die  
  - Gain that many points

---

## ⏱️ Mini‑Game Tile (Timer Challenge)
- Player draws a card from the **Question Deck**
- A **15‑second timer** starts
- Player must answer the question clearly and confidently
- Other players judge whether the answer is acceptable
- **Failure** → Lose **10 Victory Points**

---

# Player Interaction

## When Two Players Land on the Same Tile
- Both players roll **1 die**
- Higher roll wins
- Winner **steals 5 Victory Points** from the loser


# Game Workflow

1. **All players start with 25 Victory Points**
2. **Players begin scattered** across different starting positions on the board
3. **Players take turns** rolling dice and performing actions based on:
   - Dice roll conditions  
   - The tile they land on  
   - Player interactions  
4. **First player to reach 50 Victory Points wins the game**
